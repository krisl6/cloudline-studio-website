1. COMPANY OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CloudLine Studio is a growth and digital transformation partner that combines
strategy and execution under one roof. The studio helps ambitious teams diagnose
what is holding them back, digitise and automate marketing and sales, connect
departments on shared systems, and build brands that perform.

Positioning: not "just designers or marketers" but strategic brand architects and
growth operators who build systems clients can own and run independently.

Founder: Kristine Ling — Digital Marketing / AI Automation Specialist.
- 9 years in digital strategy across 5 markets (Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore,
  Australia, China).
- Drove a peak revenue lift of 300% and a 40% manpower reduction via AI automation.
- Led cross-functional teams of up to 20 people; speaker at International Women's
  Day conferences and industry events.
- Trilingual: English (native), Mandarin, Malay (professional).

Stated track record (from company materials): 7+ years, 120-200+ brands/projects,
98% client retention, work spanning e-commerce, high-ticket B2B, and SaaS.

Contact / booking: WhatsApp (wa.link/fwi8af). Website operates in EN / MS / ZH.

2. THE PROBLEM CLOUDLINE SOLVES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Businesses drowning in generic, cookie-cutter branding that blends into the
  background and forces competition on price alone.
- Agencies focused on quick deliverables (a logo, a website) then disappearing —
  leaving no strategic foundation.

3. SERVICE LINES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A) GROWTH & DIGITAL MARKETING (core retainer business)
   Four compounding services:

   1. Consultation — "Clarity before spend."
      Funnel, tooling and operations audit end to end; a prioritised, highest-
      impact roadmap; hands-on coaching; tool/budget recommendations; clear
      metrics to track progress.

   2. Marketing & Sales Digital Transformation — "Digitise, automate, unify."
      Workflow automation, tool consolidation/migration, unified single-source-
      of-truth data, AI built into the workflow, documented repeatable processes.

   3. Interdepartmental Synchronization — "One team, one view."
      Shared systems across departments, real-time role-based dashboards, clear
      handoff/ownership rules, aligned KPIs, cross-team workflow integration.

   4. Digital Marketing & Branding — "Distinctive and performing."
      Brand identity and positioning; search + social performance campaigns
      (Google, Meta, TikTok, XHS); content strategy and production; creative that
      converts; transparent reporting and optimisation.

   Productised packages currently marketed:
   - Performance Marketing (Google/Meta/TikTok) — from RM1,200/mo
   - Website Design (14-day delivery) — from RM2,560
   - Social Media Marketing (most popular) — from RM2,080/mo
   - Influencer Collaboration (5 reels + 10 stories) — from RM6,000/mo

   B2B retainer model (this proposal):
   - Setup Fee: RM10,000 (one-time onboarding, audit, systems + brand foundation)
   - Monthly Retainer: scaled to package and media budget

B) EVENT PLANNING & SPONSORSHIPS (experiential growth)
   Curated, experiential events that put a product directly into the hands of a
   matched audience, generating same-day sales plus honest, first-hand feedback.
   One flat fee, fully handled end to end: concept & curation, venue, hosting/emcee,
   organising & logistics, refreshments, curated guest list, promotion, and
   feedback capture.

   Two offerings:
   - Product Inclusion (in a CloudLine workshop event) — from USD650 (RM2,700).
     Your product featured within a larger workshop. Limited to 45 minutes.
   - Business Events (renamed from "Product Events") — from USD1,250 (approx.
     RM5,100). A dedicated event built to get users in to try and love your
     product. Up to 6 hours of immersive product time.

   Both formats guarantee that attendees try the product, give structured feedback,
   and surface real use cases — the depth and dwell time differ by format.

   Proven event examples (from company materials):
   - Vyne Wine (F&B wine tasting, 30 pax) — RM10,000 in sales in one day.
   - MonstarX (SaaS AI app builder, 35 pax) — attendees built apps live.
   - LSIGraph / SurgeGraph (SEO/AEO workshop, 50 pax) — 50 marketers trained.
   - SheSeen (fashion pop-up, Publika) — high, well-matched foot traffic.
   - Prouvers Sdn Bhd (B2B automation workshop) — teams automated content,
     replies and reporting.

   To book, a client provides: business deck (USPs + product explanation),
   Ideal Customer Profile / target demographic, timeline & expectations, and budget.
