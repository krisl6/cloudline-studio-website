# CloudLine Studio website

Read `requirement.md` before touching service copy, testimonials, or pricing — it's the living
spec and source of truth for this repo, not a one-off doc.

Two rules that come from real past incidents, not just style preference:

1. **Service taxonomy.** CloudLine has exactly 5 services (see `requirement.md` §2). Don't
   introduce a new/different list on any page — it's happened multiple times and always needs
   unwinding later. If a page needs a shorter or longer description, condense the same 5 items;
   don't invent new categories.
2. **Testimonials and case-study numbers must be real and attributable.** No composite or
   anonymised "client in X industry" stories presented as proof, no invented verification
   claims. See `requirement.md` §4-5 for what happened the one time this slipped (8 of 11
   testimonials turned out to be undisclosed placeholders while the page claimed "every
   testimonial is verified").
